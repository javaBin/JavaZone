Norway is a strange country. And so is our conferences! Every year a group of volunteers called javaBin comes together to create a different conference, something uniquely norwegian.

We invite more than 150 speakers to enlighten us for three full days. The first day is dedicated to workhops, then we have two full days with talks as well as workshops. Want to see what all the fuss is about? Well, come join us then!

What, when, how, whyyy? You know, all the practicalities…

Where's the conference at, yo?

The venue is literary a 2 minutes to walk from the main train station, bus station, trams and taxis (duh!)

Feeling lost? Use the Travel planner, choose "Oslo Spektrum" as the destination.

"Uten mat og drikke, duger helten ikke"

That's norwegian for "where can I get some food?". The nice thing about JavaZone is that we have the best food of any conference, ever! (we promise)

The Flying Culinary Circus are preparing food all day long. No queues, no price, all inclusive, all day long (from 10am)

When tired, where to sleep?

There will be beanbags at the conference. If you prefer a hotel, there are plenty of them in the area around the conference center.

Just search for hotels near "Oslo Central Station", and you should be home free!

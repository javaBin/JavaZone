The venue is a 2 minutes walk from the main train station & bus station. In the middle of Oslo!

Presentations & Workshops & Lightning Talks
Together in harmony!

JavaZone has a few different concepts when it comes to the talks. This means you have something nice to attend both when you just need a little teaser, and when you need the full package! Let's introduce you to our different formats.

60 min presentations
The talks at JavaZone are 60 minutes each, and is perfect if you want to get a good grasp on a subject. Not too long, but not too narrow either.
10 min lightning talks
This is where the experimentation happens. These talks are sharp, short and often a bit witty. Perfect if you want to get a lot of inspiration in a short time.
2-8 hour workshops
Dive deep into a subject, and learn by doing it hands-on. 4 and 8 hour workshops on September 9th, 2 hour workshops during the conference.

Well, about those workshops…

Workshops are a pretty new concept for us. Last year was the beta-release for pre-conference workshops @ JavaZone, and based on the success last year we go all in with our shiny 1.0-release this year!

The key thing to know is that Tuesday the 9th of September is reserved exclusively for workshops. This day there will be both 4-hour workshops and 8-hour workshops. September 10-11th will also have workshops, these will be 2 hours long.

We want to give you a great conference, so all workshops are included in the price! Pretty great, eh? The only thing you need to do is register. Registration opens soon!

What is JavaZone?

JavaZone is a conference organized by and for a great community of developers. The conference has been held in Oslo for 12 consecutive years.

The conference is not possible without the involvement and support of our partners. Each year, a lot of partners attend JavaZone and set up a stand for their company in our exhibition space. The exhibitions area at JavaZone is placed in the center of the conference hall, and there are always a lot of people hanging around, mingling and eating there, making this the perfect area to meet both new and old coworkers.

Our partners also get reduced prices on the tickets for JavaZone, and most send quite a lot of their people to JavaZone to enjoy the wonderful learning-opportunity our many speakers present.

Which ticket should you buy?

JavaZone sells two ticket types: tickets for members of javaBin, and tickets for anyone else. Both tickets give you the same full access to the conference, but member tickets are discounted NOK 1000.

Anyone is welcome to join javaBin, we already have more than 2.000 members from all over the country. We fully recommend that you consider a membership before buying your ticket.

The price for javaBin memberships is just NOK 500 pr. year, and can be bought in the javaBin webshop. If you do your calculations, we think that you will consider this a great deal!

What is javaBin?

javaBin is the community behind JavaZone. We are a non-profit & independent Java User Group in Norway.

The purpose is to promote Java expertise among members, member firms and the market. javaBin is headquartered in Oslo, with subgroups in Stavanger, Bergen, Trondheim, Grimstad and in southern Norway.

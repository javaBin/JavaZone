JavaZone Academy
– a free taste of JavaZone for all students –

Calling all IT-students!

Are you a student? Interested in IT? Come join us for JavaZone Academy 2015. JavaZone Academy is an event for students where we invite you to take part of JavaZone for a full day. You will get a ticket that gives you full access to the whole conference for one day with the same rights as every other participant. Get access to great talks, food, mingling and our great partners for a whole day – a great taste of the full JavaZone Experience.

Time and place

The event will take place in Oslo Spektrum during the second day of JavaZone – Thursday September 10th 2015.

Registration

The event is free, but we have a limited number of tickets availiable – we'll invite around 200 students. You have to apply for a ticket, and we'll let you know if you get one as soon as possible.

Who can attend JavaZone Academy?

JavaZone Academy is offered for free to all students. As long as you are currently a student you are welcome to attend JavaZone Academy.

What is the price for JavaZone Academy?

JavaZone Academy is free for all students. Remember to register for the event as you will need a ticket to attend.

What about other student cities in Norway?

For 2014 we have prioritized Oslo, Bergen and Trondheim. If you think that this event should take place in your city as well, please let us know at javazone@java.no.

Who will present at JavaZone Academy?

The speakers at JavaZone Academy in Bergen and Trondheim will be picked from the speakers at JavaZone. We'll announce the speakers in September. For Oslo, the regular schedule applies.

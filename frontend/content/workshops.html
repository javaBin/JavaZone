Workshops

If you're more into hands-on experiences, the JavaZone workshops might be something for you. This year, as last year, we have a full day dedicated to workshops before JavaZone officially begins. We also have a few, shorter workshops during the two days of presentations.

There are a limited number of seats for the workshops, so registration is required. First come, first served. Registration opens Tuesday August 18th at 12.00.
